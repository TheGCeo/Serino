Personalised Learning

YOUR PLAN.
BUILT AROUND
YOU.

Serinou doesn't give everyone the same experience. It learns your routines, measures your attention span, and builds a daily learning plan that's uniquely yours — adapting in real time so you always get the most out of every moment.

  • Routine Detection Learns when you pick up your phone throughout the day and schedules cards around your natural habits.
  • Attention Span Mapping Tracks response times and accuracy to identify when your brain is sharpest — then serves harder cards at your peak.
  • Adaptive Difficulty Card complexity adjusts automatically. If you're tired, simpler review. If you're sharp, new material that pushes your limits.
  • Daily Plan Rebuild Every morning your plan is recalculated. Yesterday's skips, your sleep pattern, your upcoming goals — all factored in.
